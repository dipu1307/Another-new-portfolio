Myself Shahid Afridi

Sahibzada Mohammad Shahid Khan Afridi, known as Shahid Afridi, is a former Pakistani cricketer and captain of the Pakistani national team. An all-rounder, Afridi bowled leg spin and was recognized for his aggressive batting. Afridi was also a world record holder, holding the record for the fastest ODI century, in 37 deliveries. The record stood for 17 years before being broken by Corey Anderson. He also holds the record for having hit the most sixes in the history of ODI cricket.
